Work Experience and the Learning Journey

I'm a teacher at heart and marketer by trade. My career spans classroom teaching, Apple retail training and customer education. In my current role, I'm a marketer. I help shape our customers' views with my background in education and training. I create clear solutions for complex problems and provide proactive support with creativity. My actions build relationships, and those relationships drive results.

My focus on meeting learners where they are and inspiring them to go farther has shaped both my educational approach and customer support philosophy. That focus, coupled with the skills I've gained in this program, has positioned me well for several exciting career directions. Roles I might enjoy include client onboarding and training, particularly for technology firms; learning experience design for educational platforms; and digital literacy education in community settings. Any of these paths would let me put my degree to work in meaningful ways that fuel both personal growth and career satisfaction.
