Learning Technologies Student | Graduating Fall 2025

Welcome! My name is Becky, and I'm passionate about creating learning experiences that truly make a difference in people's lives and careers.

This website showcases my journey through the Learning Technologies and Design program at the University of Missouri. Through the program, I've developed expertise in researching learner needs, designing engaging online instruction, and developing game-based learning to create memorable training experiences. As someone who believes in meeting learners where they are and inspiring them to go farther, I've built this portfolio to demonstrate how my education has sharpened both my professional capabilities and my personal approach to solving complex problems.

My background as a teacher-turned-marketer influenced my project choices throughout the program. The artifacts I've selected highlight core competencies in instructional design and technology integration while demonstrating my philosophy of creating clear, practical solutions that facilitate authentic learning. These projects showcase how the ISLT program has reinforced my passion for transforming educational challenges into engaging, results-driven experiences that help people and organizations thrive.
